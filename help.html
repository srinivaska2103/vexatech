Frequently Asked Questions

What services does VEXA TECH offer?
We specialize in end-to-end digital solutions:
  • Web Development: Custom websites and web apps.
  • UI/UX Design: User-friendly interfaces and prototypes.
  • Digital Marketing: SEO, Social Media, and Branding.
  • Business Analysis: Data insights for growth.

How much does a website cost?
Pricing depends on the complexity of the project. A simple landing page starts lower than a full e-commerce platform. We offer a Free Consultation to assess your needs and provide a custom quote.

How long does it take to complete a project?
Typically, a standard business website takes 2-4 weeks from design to launch. More complex applications may take 6-8 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline before starting.

Do you offer support after the project is finished?
Yes! We offer various maintenance packages to keep your website secure, updated, and running smoothly. We also provide 30 days of free bug-fixing support after launch.
